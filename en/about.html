A bit about me...

I'm Logan, a Computer Science student at Santa Clara University.

I have been writing code for over 6 years. Ever since I began developing my first applications, I fell in love with programming.

Growing up, I had a strong passion for creation. From LEGOs to Minecraft, I loved building and creating worlds of my own imagination. There was just something so satisfying and rewarding about being able to construct anything that my imagination desired.

Fast forward to now, and I still find myself holding that same passion for creation. Provided this, I've felt that Computer Science is the perfect discipline for me to reflect this creative liberty.

My passions lie within medicine and UI design. I would love to be a part of a development team that worked to create new medical technologies, or other interfaces that users may interact with.
